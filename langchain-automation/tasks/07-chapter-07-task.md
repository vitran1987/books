# Task: Write Chapter 7 - Future-Proofing Strategy

**Chapter Title:** Chiến Lược Tương Lai & Con Đường Phía Trước
**Objective:** Prepare the founder for the rapid evolution of AI.

## Detailed Outline & Requirements

### 1. Sự hàng hóa hóa của LLMs (Commoditization)
-   **Insight:** Models are getting cheaper and better. The "moat" is not the model.
-   **Strategy:** Build the moat around **Proprietary Data** and **Unique Workflows**.

### 2. Vertical Agents
-   **Trend:** General purpose agents (ChatGPT) vs Specialized Agents (Math Tutor for Grade 5 Vietnam).
-   **Opportunity:** Niche down. Build the best agent for a specific problem.

### 3. The One-Person Unicorn
-   **Vision:** The possibility of a billion-dollar company with < 10 employees.
-   **Roadmap:** Scale revenue without scaling headcount.

### 4. Lời khuyên cuối cùng: Agile Mindset
-   **Advice:** Don't fall in love with your code. Be ready to throw it away when a new model capability replaces it (e.g., RAG might be replaced by massive context windows).

## Tone & Style
-   **Visionary:** Big picture thinking.
-   **Encouraging:** Empower the reader to take action.
