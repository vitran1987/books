### AI Management Bias Assessment Framework
**Identify and correct bias in:**

1. **Source Bias Analysis**
   - AI company promotional material and management success story bias
   - Management consulting case study and organizational success bias
   - Survivorship bias in AI management success examples
   - Selection bias in team building and culture examples
   - Confirmation bias in AI management strategy analysis

2. **Performance Bias Correction**
   - Attribution bias in AI management success factors
   - Recency bias in management trend identification
   - Correlation vs. causation in AI management performance
   - Sample size and statistical significance issues in organizational data
   - Measurement bias in AI management and team metrics

3. **Strategy Bias Assessment**
   - Industry or vertical bias in AI management approaches
   - Company size or stage bias in management strategy examples
   - Geographic or cultural bias in organizational tactics
   - Technology or platform bias in management recommendations
   - Management style or approach bias in strategy selection
