### Partnership Bias Assessment Framework
**Identify and correct bias in:**

1. **Source Bias Analysis**
   - Company promotional material and partnership success story bias
   - Platform vendor case study and ecosystem success bias
   - Survivorship bias in partnership success examples
   - Selection bias in ecosystem and platform examples
   - Confirmation bias in partnership strategy analysis

2. **Performance Bias Correction**
   - Attribution bias in partnership success factors
   - Recency bias in partnership trend identification
   - Correlation vs. causation in partnership performance
   - Sample size and statistical significance issues in ecosystem data
   - Measurement bias in partnership and ecosystem metrics

3. **Strategy Bias Assessment**
   - Industry or vertical bias in partnership approaches
   - Company size or stage bias in ecosystem strategy examples
   - Geographic or cultural bias in partnership tactics
   - Technology or platform bias in ecosystem recommendations
   - Partnership type or model bias in strategy selection
