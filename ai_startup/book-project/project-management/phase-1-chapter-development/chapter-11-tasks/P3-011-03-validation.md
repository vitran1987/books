## üîç Expert Validation Requirements

### Marketing Source Authentication Framework
**Verify credibility and authority of:**

1. **Primary Marketing Sources**
   - Company marketing reports and performance disclosures
   - Official marketing case studies and campaign results
   - Executive marketing presentations and strategy documents
   - Marketing platform analytics and performance data
   - Customer acquisition and retention metrics

2. **Secondary Marketing Sources**
   - Marketing industry reports and research studies
   - Marketing technology vendor case studies and benchmarks
   - Marketing conference presentations and thought leadership
   - Marketing blog content and expert interviews
   - Academic marketing research and studies

3. **Performance Data Sources**
   - Marketing analytics platform data and reports
   - Customer acquisition cost and lifetime value calculations
   - Conversion rate and funnel performance metrics
   - Growth rate and retention statistics
   - ROI and marketing attribution data

### Marketing Performance Verification Protocol
**Systematically verify:**

1. **Customer Acquisition Metrics**
   - Customer acquisition cost (CAC) calculations and methodology
   - Conversion rate claims and funnel performance data
   - Channel attribution and multi-touch attribution accuracy
   - Lead quality and qualification metrics
   - Sales cycle length and conversion timeline data

2. **Growth and Retention Performance**
   - Growth rate calculations and measurement methodology
   - Customer lifetime value (CLV) calculations and assumptions
   - Retention rate and churn rate accuracy
   - Viral coefficient and referral program performance
   - Product-led growth metrics and user activation rates

3. **Marketing ROI and Effectiveness**
   - Return on marketing investment calculations
   - Marketing attribution and channel effectiveness
   - Campaign performance and optimization results
   - A/B testing results and statistical significance
   - Marketing efficiency and productivity metrics

### Marketing Bias Assessment Framework
**Identify and correct bias in:**

1. **Source Bias Analysis**
   - Company promotional material and marketing claims bias
   - Vendor case study and success story bias
   - Survivorship bias in marketing success examples
   - Selection bias in channel and strategy examples
   - Confirmation bias in marketing strategy analysis

2. **Performance Bias Correction**
   - Attribution bias in marketing success factors
   - Recency bias in marketing trend identification
   - Correlation vs. causation in marketing performance
   - Sample size and statistical significance issues
   - Measurement bias in marketing metrics

3. **Strategy Bias Assessment**
   - Industry or vertical bias in marketing approaches
   - Company size or stage bias in strategy examples
   - Geographic or cultural bias in marketing tactics
   - Technology or platform bias in tool recommendations
   - Budget or resource bias in strategy selection
