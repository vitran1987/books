## Framework 2: Bias Detection and Mitigation

### 2.1 Comprehensive Bias Assessment Framework

#### Multi-Layer Bias Detection System
**Bias Identification and Measurement Process:**
```
Data Bias Assessment:
□ Historical Bias Detection
  - Training data representativeness and balance analysis
  - Historical discrimination and systemic bias identification
  - Data collection methodology and source bias evaluation
  - Temporal bias and data currency assessment

□ Sampling and Selection Bias Analysis
  - Population representation and coverage evaluation
  - Selection criteria and methodology bias assessment
  - Geographic and demographic coverage analysis
  - Accessibility and participation barrier identification

□ Labeling and Annotation Bias Evaluation
  - Annotator bias and consistency assessment
  - Labeling guideline and criteria evaluation
  - Inter-annotator agreement and reliability measurement
  - Cultural and contextual bias in labeling identification

Algorithmic Bias Assessment:
□ Model Performance Bias Analysis
  - Performance disparity across demographic groups
  - Accuracy, precision, recall variation measurement
  - False positive and false negative rate analysis
  - Calibration and reliability assessment across groups

□ Feature and Representation Bias Evaluation
  - Feature selection and engineering bias assessment
  - Representation learning and embedding bias analysis
  - Proxy discrimination and indirect bias identification
  - Intersectional bias and complex interaction analysis

□ Decision Boundary and Threshold Bias Analysis
  - Decision threshold optimization and fairness tradeoffs
  - Classification boundary bias and group impact
  - Ranking and recommendation bias assessment
  - Resource allocation and opportunity distribution analysis

Bias Measurement Metrics:
- Demographic Parity: Equal positive prediction rates across groups
- Equalized Odds: Equal true positive and false positive rates
- Calibration: Equal prediction accuracy across groups
- Individual Fairness: Similar individuals receive similar treatment
```
