#### Objective K1: Exit Strategy Planning and Preparation Mastery
**Learning Goal**: Entrepreneurs will demonstrate comprehensive understanding of AI exit strategy planning, preparation methodologies, and execution approaches as of July 2025.

**Specific Knowledge Requirements**:
- Identify and explain different exit strategy types (IPO, acquisition, merger, management buyout)
- Understand exit timing analysis and market readiness assessment methodologies
- Recognize exit preparation requirements and due diligence readiness criteria
- Comprehend stakeholder alignment and communication strategies during exit processes
- Know exit market analysis and buyer identification approaches

**Assessment Criteria**:
- Can accurately describe and compare different exit strategy types and their applications
- Demonstrates understanding of exit timing and market readiness assessment
- Explains exit preparation and due diligence readiness requirements
- Articulates stakeholder alignment and communication strategies

**Practical Application**: Enables informed exit strategy selection and preparation planning
