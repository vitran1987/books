# Competitive Research Questions Catalog

**Purpose**: This catalog contains 20 specific, actionable research questions designed to guide comprehensive investigation of AI competitive dynamics, Big Tech strategies, and startup positioning opportunities.

## üè¢ Category 1: Big Tech Strategy Questions

### Question 1: Big Tech AI Positioning and Market Strategy
**Research Question**: How are Big Tech companies (Google, Microsoft, Amazon, Meta, Apple) positioning their AI offerings in 2025, and what are the key differentiators in their go-to-market strategies across enterprise and consumer segments?

**Investigation Focus**:
- Core AI product portfolio and strategic positioning analysis
- Enterprise vs. consumer market approach and resource allocation
- Competitive differentiation strategies and unique value propositions
- Market penetration tactics and customer acquisition approaches
- Brand positioning and thought leadership strategies

### Question 2: Big Tech Competitive Moat Development
**Research Question**: What specific competitive moats are Big Tech companies building around their AI offerings, and how sustainable are these advantages against startup competition and emerging technologies?

**Investigation Focus**:
- Data network effects and proprietary dataset advantages
- Infrastructure and compute resource competitive barriers
- Ecosystem integration and platform lock-in strategies
- Talent acquisition and research capability advantages
- Regulatory compliance and policy influence capabilities

### Question 3: Big Tech Vulnerability Analysis
**Research Question**: Where are Big Tech companies most vulnerable to startup competition, and what specific market segments, customer needs, or technology gaps present the greatest opportunities for disruption?

**Investigation Focus**:
- Product capability limitations and performance gaps
- Customer dissatisfaction and unmet need identification
- Market segment neglect and underserved customer analysis
- Technology stack limitations and innovation bottlenecks
- Organizational constraints and strategic blind spots

### Question 4: Regional Big Tech Strategy Differences
**Research Question**: How do Big Tech AI strategies differ by region and market (US, Europe, Asia), and what regulatory, cultural, or competitive factors drive these strategic variations?

**Investigation Focus**:
- Regional market entry and localization strategies
- Regulatory compliance and policy adaptation approaches
- Local partnership and ecosystem development strategies
- Cultural adaptation and customer behavior considerations
- Competitive landscape differences and regional player responses

### Question 5: Big Tech Partnership and Ecosystem Strategies
**Research Question**: What partnership strategies are Big Tech companies employing to strengthen their AI market positions, and how do these alliances impact competitive dynamics and startup opportunities?

**Investigation Focus**:
- Strategic partnership selection criteria and objectives
- Ecosystem development and third-party integration strategies
- Acquisition targets and consolidation patterns
- Developer community engagement and platform strategies
- Competitive response to partnership announcements
