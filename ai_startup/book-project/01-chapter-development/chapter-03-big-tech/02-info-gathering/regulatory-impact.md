### EU AI Act Implementation and Competitive Effects

**Regulatory Framework Overview**
- **EU AI Act**: Comprehensive AI regulation adopted June 2024, full implementation by 2025-2027
- **Risk-Based Approach**: Tiered regulation based on AI system risk levels
- **Compliance Requirements**: Mandatory conformity assessments, documentation, and monitoring
- **Enforcement Mechanisms**: Significant penalties up to 7% of global annual turnover
- **Market Impact**: Creating compliance costs and competitive advantages for prepared companies

**Competitive Advantages for EU Companies**
- **Regulatory Expertise**: European companies built with AI Act compliance from inception
- **Data Sovereignty**: EU data residency requirements favoring European providers
- **Government Procurement**: Preference for compliant European AI solutions in public sector
- **Market Access**: Easier access to EU market through native compliance capabilities
