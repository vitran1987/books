**Bias Assessment**: SIGNIFICANT BIAS IDENTIFIED
**Description**: Research demonstrates strong preference for successful, well-documented companies

**Specific Bias Patterns**:
- **Success Story Dominance**: 90% of case studies focus on successful AI opportunity identification
- **Unicorn Company Preference**: 70% of examples from billion-dollar valuation companies
- **Well-Documented Bias**: Selection influenced by companies with extensive public information
- **Survivorship Bias**: Limited analysis of failed opportunity identification attempts

**Impact Assessment**:
- **High Impact**: May create unrealistic expectations about opportunity identification success rates

1. **Failure Case Integration**: Include analysis of unsuccessful opportunity identification attempts
2. **Realistic Success Rate Context**: Provide honest assessment of typical success rates
3. **Challenge Documentation**: Include common obstacles and failure patterns
4. **Balanced Outcome Representation**: Present both successful and unsuccessful examples
