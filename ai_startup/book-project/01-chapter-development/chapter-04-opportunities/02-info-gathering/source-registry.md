- **Verification**: Multiple source confirmation, expert validation, track record assessment
- **Usage**: Primary sources for critical decisions and strategic analysis
- **Reliability**: 95%+ accuracy and credibility

**MEDIUM Authority Sources**
- **Criteria**: Industry publications, expert commentary, specialized research organizations
- **Examples**: Industry trade publications, expert blogs, specialized consultancies
- **Verification**: Cross-reference with high authority sources, expert review
- **Usage**: Supporting information and trend identification
- **Reliability**: 80-90% accuracy with expert validation
