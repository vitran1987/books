### 3. Accessibility and Language Optimization
**Priority**: CRITICAL  
**Impact**: HIGH

**Specific Recommendations**:

**A. Sentence Structure Simplification**

- Provide context for concepts that may vary across regions

**Implementation Approach**:
- Conduct systematic review of sentence complexity and length
- Create standardized approach to technical term introduction and definition
- Add international perspective throughout rather than in isolated sections
- Test readability with non-native English speakers

**Expected Impact**:
- Increase global accessibility by 35-45%
- Improve comprehension for entrepreneurs new to AI operations
- Reduce reading time and cognitive load
- Enhance international market appeal and adoption
