### KO1: AI Development Cost Accounting Fundamentals
**Objective**: Understand the unique accounting requirements and challenges for AI development costs and asset capitalization.

**Learning Outcomes**:
- Comprehend ASC 350-40 application to AI model development and capitalization criteria
- Understand data asset recognition, valuation, and accounting treatment
- Recognize AI infrastructure cost allocation and capitalization decisions
- Grasp third-party AI service cost accounting and contract considerations
- Understand AI asset impairment testing and useful life determination

**Assessment Criteria**:
- Can articulate AI development cost capitalization criteria and decision frameworks
- Demonstrates understanding of data asset accounting and valuation methodologies
- Explains AI infrastructure cost allocation and operational vs. development classification
- Identifies appropriate accounting treatment for various AI development scenarios

**Assessment Criteria**:
