### Framework Component 3: Equity Compensation Accounting System

#### ASC 718 Compliance for AI Startups

**Phase 1: Equity Compensation Program Design (Week 1-2)**

**Step 1: Equity Instrument Classification**
1. **Stock Option Programs**:
   - **Incentive Stock Options (ISOs)**: Tax-advantaged employee options
   - **Non-Qualified Stock Options (NQSOs)**: Flexible option structures
   - **Early Exercise Options**: Options exercisable before vesting
   - **Reload Options**: Automatic option grants upon exercise

2. **Restricted Stock Programs**:
   - **Restricted Stock Awards (RSAs)**: Direct stock grants with restrictions
   - **Restricted Stock Units (RSUs)**: Promise to deliver stock upon vesting
   - **Performance Stock Units (PSUs)**: Performance-based stock compensation
   - **Stock Appreciation Rights (SARs)**: Cash or stock-settled appreciation rights

**Step 2: Valuation Methodology Framework**
1. **Fair Value Determination**:
   - **409A Valuations**: Independent third-party valuations for private companies
   - **Black-Scholes Model**: Option pricing model for standard options
   - **Monte Carlo Simulation**: Complex option and performance award valuation
   - **Market Approach**: Comparable company and transaction analysis

2. **Valuation Input Assessment**:
   - **Stock Price**: Current fair value of underlying stock
   - **Exercise Price**: Strike price for option exercises
   - **Volatility**: Expected stock price volatility over option term
   - **Risk-Free Rate**: Treasury rate matching option expected term
   - **Dividend Yield**: Expected dividend payments during option term

**Phase 2: Expense Recognition and Reporting (Week 3-4)**

**Step 3: Expense Recognition Framework**
1. **Graded Vesting Method**:
   - Separate expense recognition for each vesting tranche
   - Accelerated expense recognition for early vesting tranches
   - Cumulative expense catch-up for performance modifications
   - Forfeiture rate estimation and adjustment

2. **Modification Accounting**:
   - Incremental fair value calculation for modifications
   - Expense acceleration for vesting modifications
   - Cancellation and replacement accounting
   - Performance condition modification treatment
