## AI Investor Reporting Framework Architecture

### Framework Component 1: Financial Reporting Standards System

#### GAAP Compliance and Financial Statement Preparation

**Phase 1: Financial Statement Framework Design (Week 1-2)**

**Step 1: AI-Specific Financial Statement Considerations**
1. **Revenue Recognition Complexity**:
   - **ASC 606 Implementation**: Multi-element AI service revenue recognition
   - **Usage-Based Revenue**: Real-time API and consumption-based revenue tracking
   - **Subscription Revenue**: Recurring AI platform and service revenue recognition
   - **Professional Services**: AI consulting and custom development revenue
   - **Performance Obligations**: Complex AI service performance obligation identification

2. **Balance Sheet Considerations**:
   - **Capitalized Development Costs**: AI development cost capitalization under ASC 350-40
   - **Intangible Assets**: AI models, algorithms, and IP asset valuation
   - **Data Assets**: Training data and proprietary dataset asset treatment
   - **Deferred Revenue**: Prepaid AI services and subscription revenue management
   - **Equity Compensation**: Stock-based compensation liability and equity treatment

**Step 2: AI-Specific Accounting Policies**
1. **Revenue Recognition Policies**:
   - **Performance Obligation Identification**: Systematic approach to identifying distinct services
   - **Transaction Price Allocation**: Methodology for allocating transaction price across obligations
   - **Revenue Recognition Timing**: Criteria for point-in-time vs. over-time recognition
   - **Variable Consideration**: Treatment of usage-based and performance-based pricing
   - **Contract Modifications**: Accounting for AI service contract changes and expansions

2. **Cost Capitalization Policies**:
   - **Development Stage Classification**: Clear criteria for capitalizable development stages
   - **Capitalizable Cost Categories**: Specific cost categories eligible for capitalization
   - **Useful Life Determination**: Methodology for determining AI asset useful lives
   - **Impairment Testing**: Regular impairment testing procedures for capitalized costs
   - **Amortization Methods**: Systematic amortization approaches for AI assets

**Phase 2: Financial Statement Preparation Process (Week 3-4)**

**Step 3: Monthly Financial Close Process**
1. **Revenue Recognition Process**:
   - **Usage Data Collection**: Automated collection of API and service usage data
   - **Revenue Calculation**: Real-time revenue calculation and recognition
   - **Deferred Revenue Analysis**: Monthly analysis of deferred revenue balances
   - **Contract Review**: Regular review of new contracts and modifications
   - **Revenue Quality Assessment**: Analysis of revenue quality and sustainability

2. **Cost Accounting Process**:
   - **Development Cost Review**: Monthly review of capitalizable development costs
   - **Expense Classification**: Proper classification of operating expenses
   - **Accrual Analysis**: Comprehensive accrual analysis and adjustment
   - **Inventory and Asset Review**: Regular review of asset balances and valuations
   - **Equity Compensation**: Monthly equity compensation expense calculation
