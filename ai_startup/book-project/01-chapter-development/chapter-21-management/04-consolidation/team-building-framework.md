### Framework Component 2: Team Composition and Structure System

#### Cross-Functional AI Team Architecture

**Phase 1: Team Structure Design (Week 1-2)**

**Step 1: Technical Core Team Structure**
1. **AI/ML Engineering Team**:
   - **Senior AI Engineers**: Lead AI model development and architecture decisions
   - **ML Engineers**: Implement and optimize machine learning models and pipelines
   - **Research Scientists**: Conduct research and develop novel AI approaches
   - **Data Scientists**: Analyze data, develop features, and validate model performance
   - **MLOps Engineers**: Manage model deployment, monitoring, and infrastructure

   **Team Composition Guidelines**:
   - 1 Senior AI Engineer per 3-4 ML Engineers
   - 1 Research Scientist per 5-6 engineering team members
   - 1 Data Scientist per 2-3 ML Engineers
   - 1 MLOps Engineer per 8-10 total technical team members

2. **Product Integration Team**:
   - **AI Product Managers**: Define AI product strategy and requirements
   - **Technical Product Owners**: Bridge technical teams and business requirements
   - **UX/UI Designers**: Design user experiences for AI-powered products
   - **Domain Experts**: Provide industry-specific knowledge and context
   - **Quality Assurance**: Test and validate AI model performance and reliability

**Step 2: Remote and Distributed Team Management**

   - **Cost Optimization**: Geographic arbitrage for talent costs

2. **Remote Team Success Strategies**:
