## Framework Customization Guidelines

### Industry-Specific Adaptations
**Retail/E-commerce**: Focus on peak season scaling and customer experience
**Manufacturing**: Emphasize 24/7 reliability and predictive maintenance
**Healthcare**: Prioritize compliance, security, and audit trail requirements
**Financial Services**: Stress regulatory compliance and risk management

### Company Size Adaptations
**Startup (1-50 employees)**: Focus on automation and efficiency
**Growth Stage (51-200 employees)**: Emphasize scalability and quality
**Enterprise (200+ employees)**: Prioritize governance and global operations

### Resource Level Adaptations
**Limited Resources**: Prioritize high-impact, low-cost implementations
**Moderate Resources**: Balance automation with strategic investments
**Abundant Resources**: Implement comprehensive frameworks with advanced features
