#### Fair Use and Copyright Strategy

**Fair Use Analysis Framework:**
Based on validated GitHub Copilot case study analysis:

1. **Purpose and Character Assessment**:
   - Commercial vs. educational use evaluation
   - Transformative nature of AI training analysis
   - Market creation vs. substitution assessment

2. **Nature of Copyrighted Work**:
   - Factual vs. creative content analysis
   - Published vs. unpublished work consideration
   - Functional vs. expressive content evaluation

3. **Amount and Substantiality**:
   - Quantitative usage assessment
   - Qualitative importance evaluation
   - Necessity for transformative purpose

4. **Market Impact Analysis**:
   - Effect on original work's market value
   - Creation of new markets vs. substitution
   - Licensing market impact assessment
