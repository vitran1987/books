## üìä Source Strategy Overview

### Source Authority Hierarchy
1. **Primary Legal Sources** (40%): Government agencies, regulatory bodies, court decisions, and official legal documentation
2. **Legal Expert and Professional Sources** (30%): Legal professionals, technology law specialists, and authoritative legal analysis
3. **Industry Implementation Sources** (20%): Successful legal implementations, case studies, and industry best practices
4. **Academic and Research Sources** (10%): Legal research institutions, academic analysis, and policy research

### Source Quality Standards
- **Legal Authority**: Sources must have recognized legal authority or expertise in technology law and IP protection
- **Currency**: All legal information must reflect current laws and precedents as of July 2025
- **Accuracy**: Legal interpretations must be verified by qualified legal professionals
- **Practical Application**: Sources must provide actionable guidance for AI legal protection implementation
- **Verification**: All legal claims must be verifiable through multiple authoritative sources

## üîç Source Research Methodology

### Source Identification and Validation Process

#### Phase 1: Source Discovery and Authority Verification (Week 1)
**Activities**:
1. **Comprehensive Source Mapping**: Systematic identification of all potential legal and IP sources
2. **Legal Authority Verification**: Validation of source legal authority and expertise in AI law
3. **Currency Assessment**: Verification that sources reflect current legal landscape as of July 2025
4. **Accessibility Confirmation**: Ensuring all sources are accessible and available for legal research
5. **Quality Scoring**: Initial assessment of source quality, authority, and relevance

#### Phase 2: Legal Content Collection and Analysis (Weeks 2-3)
**Activities**:
1. **Systematic Legal Research**: Organized collection of legal guidance, case law, and regulatory requirements
2. **Legal Accuracy Verification**: Validation of legal interpretations and regulatory compliance requirements
3. **Cross-Reference Validation**: Verification of legal information consistency across multiple authoritative sources
4. **Gap Identification**: Identification of areas requiring additional legal research or expert consultation
5. **Expert Legal Review**: Legal professional review of collected information and interpretations

#### Phase 3: Source Integration and Legal Synthesis (Week 4)
**Activities**:
1. **Legal Information Synthesis**: Integration of legal information from multiple sources into comprehensive guidance
2. **Legal Conflict Resolution**: Resolution of conflicting legal information through additional research and expert consultation
3. **Practical Implementation Development**: Translation of legal requirements into practical implementation guidance
4. **Legal Quality Assurance**: Final validation of all legal source materials and interpretations
5. **Legal Documentation Completion**: Comprehensive documentation of all legal sources and research methodology

#### Legal Authority Validation
**Criteria**:
- [ ] Source has recognized legal authority in AI law, IP protection, or technology regulation
- [ ] Legal interpretations are current and accurate as of July 2025
- [ ] Information is consistent with official legal guidance and court precedents

#### Practical Implementation Validation
**Criteria**:
- [ ] Source provides actionable legal implementation guidance for AI entrepreneurs
- [ ] Legal information is relevant to AI business development and IP protection
