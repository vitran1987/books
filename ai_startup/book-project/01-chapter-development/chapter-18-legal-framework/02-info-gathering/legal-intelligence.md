**Strategic Patent Filing Approaches:**
1. **Core Algorithm Protection**: Patenting fundamental AI algorithms and mathematical models

3. **System Architecture Patents**: Covering AI system designs and infrastructure approaches

- **Portfolio Development**: Build comprehensive patent portfolios covering multiple aspects of AI technology
- **Defensive Publications**: Use defensive publications to prevent competitors from patenting obvious improvements
