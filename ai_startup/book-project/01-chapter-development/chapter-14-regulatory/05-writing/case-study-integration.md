## Cross-Case Pattern Analysis

### Success Pattern Integration
**Common Success Factors Across Cases**:

1. **Early Regulatory Engagement**
   - Proactive consultation with regulatory authorities
   - Relationship building and trust development
   - Regulatory feedback integration into development
   - Early identification of compliance requirements

2. **Expert Capability and Resources**
   - Dedicated compliance expertise and leadership
   - Adequate resource allocation and budget commitment
   - External advisory and consultant engagement
   - Continuous capability development

3. **Systematic Implementation Approach**
   - Comprehensive compliance framework and methodology
   - Clear timeline and milestone management
   - Quality management system integration
   - Systematic documentation and evidence development

4. **Stakeholder Engagement and Communication**
   - Transparent communication with all stakeholders
   - Proactive issue identification and resolution
   - Trust building through consistent engagement
   - Feedback integration and continuous improvement

### Failure Pattern Integration
**Common Failure Factors Across Cases**:

1. **Reactive Compliance Approach**
   - Addressing compliance only after regulatory scrutiny
   - Insufficient proactive risk assessment
   - Poor regulatory relationship management
   - Inadequate preparation for requirements

2. **Inadequate Expertise and Resources**
   - Insufficient compliance and regulatory expertise
   - Underestimated resource requirements
   - Limited external advisory engagement
   - Poor capability development

3. **Poor Planning and Execution**
   - Inadequate compliance planning and timeline management
   - Insufficient documentation and evidence development
   - Poor quality management and process control
   - Inadequate stakeholder engagement
