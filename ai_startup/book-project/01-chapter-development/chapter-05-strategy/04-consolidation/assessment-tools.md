## Tool 3: Strategic Gap Analysis

### 3.1 Strategic Gap Identification

**Gap Analysis Framework**:

1. **Vision-Reality Gap**
   - **Current State**: Where the company is today
   - **Desired State**: Strategic vision and goals
   - **Gap Size**: Magnitude of difference
   - **Gap Priority**: Importance and urgency of closing gap
   - **Gap Action Plan**: Specific actions to close gap

2. **Capability-Requirement Gap**
   - **Current Capabilities**: Existing organizational capabilities
   - **Required Capabilities**: Capabilities needed for strategic success
   - **Capability Gap**: Missing or insufficient capabilities
   - **Development Plan**: Plan to build or acquire needed capabilities
   - **Timeline**: Timeline for capability development

3. **Resource-Need Gap**
   - **Current Resources**: Available financial, human, and technology resources
   - **Resource Requirements**: Resources needed for strategic execution
   - **Resource Gap**: Shortfall in required resources
   - **Resource Plan**: Plan to acquire or optimize resources
   - **Investment Priority**: Priority order for resource investment

### 4.2 Risk Mitigation Planning

**Risk Mitigation Framework**:

1. **High-Priority Risks (Score 15-25)**
   - **Immediate Action Required**: Develop comprehensive mitigation plans
   - **Monitoring Frequency**: Weekly monitoring and review
   - **Escalation Process**: Executive-level escalation and decision-making
   - **Contingency Planning**: Detailed contingency and backup plans
   - **Resource Allocation**: Dedicated resources for risk mitigation

2. **Medium-Priority Risks (Score 8-14)**
   - **Planned Mitigation**: Develop mitigation plans with timeline
   - **Monitoring Frequency**: Monthly monitoring and review
   - **Management Process**: Management-level oversight and coordination
   - **Preparation Planning**: Prepare response plans and resources
   - **Regular Assessment**: Quarterly risk assessment and plan updates

3. **Low-Priority Risks (Score 1-7)**
   - **Monitoring Only**: Regular monitoring without active mitigation
   - **Monitoring Frequency**: Quarterly monitoring and review
   - **Awareness Maintenance**: Maintain awareness and basic preparation
   - **Threshold Monitoring**: Monitor for changes in risk level
   - **Annual Review**: Annual review and reassessment
