## Component 2: Technical Evaluation and Assessment

### 2.1 AI Technical Assessment Framework

#### Multi-Stage Evaluation Process
**Stage 1: Resume and Portfolio Screening (30 minutes):**
```
Technical Background Evaluation:
□ Educational credentials: PhD/MS in AI/ML, CS, or related field
□ Research experience: Publications, citations, conference presentations
□ Industry experience: Relevant AI/ML roles and project contributions
□ Technical skills: Programming languages, frameworks, tools

Portfolio Assessment:
□ GitHub contributions: Code quality, project complexity, collaboration
□ Research publications: Impact factor, citation count, novelty
□ Open source contributions: Community engagement and leadership
□ Technical writing: Blog posts, documentation, tutorials

Screening Criteria:
- Education: Advanced degree in relevant field (preferred)
- Experience: 2+ years of AI/ML development or research
- Publications: 3+ peer-reviewed papers or equivalent contributions
- Code quality: Clean, well-documented, and maintainable code

Pass Rate: 20% of applicants advance to technical phone screen
```

**Stage 2: Technical Phone Screen (60 minutes):**
```
Technical Knowledge Assessment:
□ AI/ML fundamentals: Algorithms, mathematics, statistics
□ Deep learning concepts: Architectures, training, optimization
□ Practical experience: Model development, deployment, debugging
□ Problem-solving: Technical challenges and solution approaches

Interview Structure:
- 10 minutes: Background and experience discussion
- 30 minutes: Technical problem-solving and coding
- 15 minutes: AI/ML concept discussion and depth assessment
- 5 minutes: Questions and next steps

Evaluation Criteria:
- Technical depth: Advanced understanding of AI/ML concepts
- Problem-solving: Systematic approach and creative solutions
- Communication: Clear explanation of technical concepts
- Coding ability: Clean, efficient, and correct implementation

Pass Rate: 40% of phone screens advance to on-site interviews
```

**Stage 3: On-Site Technical Interviews (4-5 hours):**
```
Interview Panel Structure:
□ AI Research Interview (90 minutes): Research experience and innovation
□ ML Engineering Interview (90 minutes): Production system development
□ System Design Interview (60 minutes): Scalable AI architecture
□ Coding Interview (60 minutes): Algorithm and data structure proficiency
□ Cultural Fit Interview (45 minutes): Values alignment and collaboration

AI Research Interview Focus:
- Research methodology and experimental design
- Novel algorithm development and optimization
- Literature review and competitive analysis
- Research-to-product transition and commercialization

ML Engineering Interview Focus:
- Production ML pipeline development and deployment
- Model optimization and performance tuning
- Data engineering and quality assurance
- MLOps and monitoring system implementation

Evaluation Scoring:
- Technical Excellence (40%): Depth and breadth of AI/ML knowledge
- Problem-Solving (30%): Systematic approach and creative solutions
- Communication (20%): Clear explanation and collaboration ability
- Cultural Fit (10%): Values alignment and team integration potential

Pass Rate: 25% of on-site candidates receive offers
```
