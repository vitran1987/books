## Component 4: Retention Strategy and Risk Management

### 4.1 Proactive Retention Management

#### Retention Risk Assessment Framework
**Early Warning System:**
```
Retention Risk Indicators:
□ Engagement survey score decline (>10% decrease)
□ Performance rating changes and trend analysis
□ Manager relationship quality and feedback patterns
□ Career progression and advancement timeline delays
□ Compensation and market competitiveness gaps

Risk Assessment Process:
- Monthly retention risk review and analysis
- Quarterly individual retention planning sessions
- Annual compensation and career progression review
- Exit interview analysis and trend identification

Intervention Strategies:
□ Manager coaching and relationship improvement
□ Career development and advancement acceleration
□ Compensation adjustment and market alignment
□ Role modification and responsibility expansion
□ Team transfer and internal mobility facilitation

Success Metrics:
- Retention rate: 90%+ annual retention for key talent
- Risk prediction accuracy: 80%+ accuracy in identifying at-risk employees
- Intervention effectiveness: 70%+ success rate in retention interventions
- Manager effectiveness: 85%+ manager approval ratings from team members
```
