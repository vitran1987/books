### üìö Knowledge Objectives

#### Objective K1: AI Talent Market and Landscape Mastery
**Learning Goal**: Entrepreneurs will demonstrate comprehensive understanding of the AI talent market, including supply dynamics, compensation trends, and competitive landscape as of July 2025.

**Specific Knowledge Requirements**:
- Identify current AI talent supply and demand patterns across different specializations and geographic markets
- Explain compensation benchmarks and market rates for all major AI roles and experience levels
- Describe emerging AI specializations and evolving skill requirements for different positions
- Analyze competitive dynamics and talent acquisition challenges in the AI market
- Understand geographic talent distribution and remote work trends affecting AI hiring

**Assessment Criteria**:
- Can accurately cite current AI talent market statistics and compensation data
- Demonstrates understanding of talent supply constraints and opportunity areas
- Explains regional variations and competitive factors affecting AI hiring
- Articulates emerging trends and future talent market evolution

**Practical Application**: Enables informed talent acquisition strategy development and competitive positioning

#### Objective K2: AI Team Structure and Organization Design Understanding
**Learning Goal**: Entrepreneurs will comprehensively understand optimal AI team structures, role definitions, and organizational design principles for different startup stages and AI applications.

**Specific Knowledge Requirements**:
- Identify optimal team compositions and role distributions for different AI startup stages
- Understand technical role definitions and responsibility mapping for AI positions
- Recognize cross-functional integration requirements and collaboration frameworks
- Know leadership hierarchy design and decision-making structure optimization
- Understand team scaling patterns and organizational evolution best practices

**Assessment Criteria**:
- Can design appropriate team structures for specific AI applications and startup stages
- Demonstrates knowledge of role definitions and responsibility frameworks
- Explains cross-functional integration and collaboration requirements
- Understands scaling challenges and organizational development needs

**Practical Application**: Enables effective team structure design and organizational planning

#### Objective K3: AI Talent Assessment and Evaluation Methodology Knowledge
**Learning Goal**: Entrepreneurs will master AI-specific talent assessment approaches, technical evaluation methods, and cultural fit assessment frameworks.

**Specific Knowledge Requirements**:
- Understand technical assessment methodologies and coding challenge design for AI roles

- Recognize portfolio evaluation criteria and project assessment methods
- Understand reference check strategies and background verification processes
- Know probationary period design and early performance evaluation frameworks

**Assessment Criteria**:
- Can design comprehensive assessment processes for different AI roles
- Demonstrates knowledge of technical and cultural evaluation methods
- Explains assessment criteria and evaluation frameworks
- Understands assessment quality and bias mitigation approaches

**Practical Application**: Enables effective candidate evaluation and hiring decision-making

#### Objective K4: AI Team Performance and Productivity Optimization Knowledge
**Learning Goal**: Entrepreneurs will understand performance measurement, productivity enhancement, and team optimization strategies specific to AI development environments.

**Specific Knowledge Requirements**:
