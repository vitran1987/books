# Burn Rate Database

## Executive Summary

This document provides comprehensive analysis of AI startup burn rate optimization strategies and cost management approaches based on current industry data as of July 2025. The database covers burn rate benchmarks, cost optimization techniques, runway management, and financial efficiency metrics for AI companies across different stages and business models.

## AI Startup Burn Rate Benchmarks and Analysis

### 1. Industry Burn Rate Benchmarks by Stage and Business Model

**Burn Rate Benchmarks by Funding Stage:**

**Pre-Seed Stage (0-6 months):**
- **Average Monthly Burn**: $50K-$150K
- **Team Size**: 2-5 employees
- **Primary Costs**: Founder salaries, cloud computing, basic tools
- **Runway Target**: 12-18 months
- **Key Focus**: Product development and initial validation

**Typical Cost Breakdown:**
- Personnel (60-70%): $30K-$105K monthly
- Cloud/Infrastructure (15-25%): $7.5K-$37.5K monthly
- Software/Tools (5-10%): $2.5K-$15K monthly
- Marketing/Sales (5-10%): $2.5K-$15K monthly
- Other Operating (5-10%): $2.5K-$15K monthly

**Seed Stage (6-18 months):**
- **Average Monthly Burn**: $200K-$500K
- **Team Size**: 5-15 employees
- **Primary Costs**: Engineering team, data costs, initial sales/marketing
- **Runway Target**: 18-24 months
- **Key Focus**: Product-market fit and early revenue

**Typical Cost Breakdown:**
- Personnel (65-75%): $130K-$375K monthly
- Cloud/Infrastructure (10-20%): $20K-$100K monthly
- Sales/Marketing (8-15%): $16K-$75K monthly
- Software/Tools (3-7%): $6K-$35K monthly
- Other Operating (5-10%): $10K-$50K monthly

**Series A Stage (18-36 months):**
- **Average Monthly Burn**: $800K-$2M
- **Team Size**: 15-50 employees
- **Primary Costs**: Scaling team, enterprise sales, infrastructure
- **Runway Target**: 24-36 months
- **Key Focus**: Revenue growth and market expansion

**Typical Cost Breakdown:**
- Personnel (60-70%): $480K-$1.4M monthly
- Sales/Marketing (15-25%): $120K-$500K monthly
- Cloud/Infrastructure (8-15%): $64K-$300K monthly
- Software/Tools (3-7%): $24K-$140K monthly
- Other Operating (5-12%): $40K-$240K monthly

**Series B+ Stage (36+ months):**
- **Average Monthly Burn**: $3M-$10M+
- **Team Size**: 50-200+ employees
- **Primary Costs**: Large teams, global expansion, enterprise infrastructure
- **Runway Target**: 18-24 months (shorter due to revenue generation)
- **Key Focus**: Profitability path and market leadership
