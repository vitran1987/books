- **Total Cost of Ownership (TCO) Analysis**: Comprehensive cost analysis including compute, storage, network, and service costs

- **Security and Compliance Capabilities**: Assessment of security features, compliance certifications, and data protection

- **Reliability and Service Level Agreements**: Evaluation of platform reliability, uptime guarantees, and SLA commitments

- **Strategic Alignment and Long-term Viability**: Analysis of platform strategic direction and long-term sustainability
