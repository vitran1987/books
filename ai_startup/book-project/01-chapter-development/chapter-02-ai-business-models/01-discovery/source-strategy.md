## üîç Source Verification and Validation Framework

### Source Credibility Assessment Criteria

**Authority Verification**
- **Expertise Validation**: Verify speaker/author credentials and domain expertise
- **Track Record Assessment**: Evaluate success history and industry recognition
- **Institutional Affiliation**: Confirm authoritative institutional backing and support
- **Peer Recognition**: Assess industry peer recognition and citation frequency

**Currency and Relevance Standards**
- **Publication Date**: Prioritize sources from 2023-2025 for current market conditions
- **Market Relevance**: Ensure information reflects current AI business model landscape
- **Technology Currency**: Verify information accounts for latest AI developments
- **Competitive Relevance**: Confirm analysis reflects current competitive dynamics

### Information Validation Methodology

**Cross-Reference Verification**
- **Multiple Source Confirmation**: Verify key information across 3+ independent sources
- **Triangulation Analysis**: Use different source types to confirm critical insights
- **Contradiction Resolution**: Investigate and resolve conflicting information
- **Bias Assessment**: Identify potential conflicts of interest and perspective limitations

**Quality Assurance Process**
- **Fact-Checking Protocol**: Verify quantitative data and specific claims
- **Source Documentation**: Maintain detailed source attribution and access information
- **Update Monitoring**: Track source updates and information currency
- **Expert Review**: Validate findings with domain experts and industry professionals

### Research Documentation Standards

**Source Attribution Requirements**
- **Complete Citation**: Full source information including date, author, publication
- **Access Information**: URLs, database access, interview details
- **Credibility Assessment**: Authority evaluation and bias identification
- **Relevance Scoring**: Information value and application assessment

**Information Organization Framework**
- **Category Classification**: Organize sources by business model category and research question
- **Priority Ranking**: Rank sources by authority, currency, and relevance
- **Cross-Reference Mapping**: Link related information across different sources
- **Update Tracking**: Monitor source updates and information evolution

This comprehensive source strategy ensures systematic collection of authoritative, current, and relevant information about AI business model innovation, providing the foundation for accurate, actionable insights that serve entrepreneur decision-making and business development success.
