## üí∞ Framework 2: AI Monetization Strategy Framework

### Pricing Model Selection and Optimization

**Revenue Model Decision Framework Based on Validated Market Performance**

**Subscription-Based Pricing (42% of AI software revenue)**
- **Optimal Use Cases**: Consistent usage patterns, ongoing value delivery
- **Performance Metrics**: $2,400 average annual revenue per user, 92% retention rate
- **Pricing Structure Strategy**:
  - Basic Tiers ($10-50/month): 65% subscribers, 35% revenue - customer acquisition focus
  - Professional Tiers ($50-200/month): 25% subscribers, 40% revenue - value optimization
  - Enterprise Tiers ($200+/month): 10% subscribers, 25% revenue - premium positioning
- **Success Factors**: Predictable value delivery, feature differentiation, upgrade pathways

**Usage-Based/API Pricing (28% of AI software revenue)**
- **Optimal Use Cases**: Variable usage patterns, developer-focused products
- **Performance Metrics**: 45% year-over-year growth, $0.002-$0.05 per API call
- **Pricing Strategy Framework**:
  - Free Tier: Customer acquisition and product validation (12% conversion rate)
  - Pay-per-Use: Transparent cost structure aligned with value delivery
  - Volume Discounts: Enterprise customer retention and expansion
- **Success Factors**: Transparent pricing, usage analytics, developer experience optimization

**Enterprise Licensing (18% of AI software revenue)**
- **Optimal Use Cases**: Large-scale deployments, custom requirements
- **Performance Metrics**: $125,000 average contract value, highest revenue per customer
- **Licensing Strategy Framework**:
  - Custom Deployment: On-premises or private cloud solutions
  - White-Label Licensing: Partner integration and reseller opportunities
  - Industry-Specific Solutions: Vertical market specialization
- **Success Factors**: Enterprise sales capability, custom implementation support, compliance features
