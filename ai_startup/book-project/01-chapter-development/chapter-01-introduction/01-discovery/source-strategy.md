## üîç Information Validation Framework

### Source Authority Assessment
- **Expertise Verification**: Confirm speaker/author credentials and track record
- **Institutional Credibility**: Assess organization reputation and methodology
- **Peer Recognition**: Review citations, references, and industry acknowledgment
- **Conflict of Interest**: Identify potential biases and commercial interests

### Recency and Relevance Validation
- **Publication Date**: Prioritize sources from 2024-2025 for current relevance
- **Update Frequency**: Monitor for newer information that supersedes older sources
- **Market Relevance**: Ensure information applies to current market conditions
- **Geographic Relevance**: Verify applicability to target markets and regions

### Information Triangulation
- **Multiple Source Confirmation**: Require 2-3 independent sources for key claims
- **Cross-Reference Validation**: Compare data across different source types
- **Expert Consensus**: Seek expert validation for controversial or uncertain claims
- **Data Consistency**: Verify statistical consistency across multiple reports

### Quality Assurance Process
- **Fact-Checking Protocol**: Systematic verification of all quantitative claims
- **Expert Review**: Industry expert validation of key findings and conclusions
- **Peer Review**: Collaborative review of research findings and interpretations
- **Continuous Updates**: Regular monitoring and updating of information as new sources emerge
