## üîç Source Verification Framework

### Verification Criteria
**Authority Assessment**:
- **Primary Sources**: Company reports, financial filings, direct statements
- **Secondary Sources**: Professional journalism, market research, academic analysis
- **Expert Sources**: Industry leaders, researchers, investors with verified credentials
- **Data Sources**: Statistical agencies, market research firms, financial databases

**Currency Validation**:
- **Recency Priority**: Information from 2024-2025 prioritized
- **Update Tracking**: Regular monitoring for newer information
- **Superseding Data**: Newer sources replace older information when available
- **Trend Validation**: Long-term trends verified across multiple time periods

**Quality Assurance**:
- **Multi-source Verification**: Key claims verified by 2-3 independent sources
- **Cross-reference Validation**: Data consistency checked across sources
- **Expert Review**: Industry expert validation for controversial claims
- **Bias Assessment**: Source bias identified and accounted for in analysis

### Information Triangulation
**Financial Data**: Company reports + financial journalism + market research
**Market Size**: Multiple research firms + academic studies + industry analysis
**Technology Trends**: Company announcements + research papers + expert interviews
**Investment Data**: VC reports + financial journalism + startup databases
