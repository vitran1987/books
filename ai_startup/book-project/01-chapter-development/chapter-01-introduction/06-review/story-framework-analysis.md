## Strategic Framework Usability Analysis

### 1. AI Opportunity Assessment Framework ✅ EXCELLENT (9/10)

**Framework Structure:**
```
Market Readiness (25% weight)
- Clear, expensive problem AI can solve
- Active customer search for solutions  
- Venture-scale market size
- Allocated customer budget

Technology Feasibility (25% weight)
- Proven, available AI technology
- Access to required data
- Manageable technical risks
- 6-12 month MVP timeline

Competitive Positioning (25% weight)
- Competition intensity assessment
- Sustainable competitive advantages
- Big Tech entry risk evaluation
- Differentiation strategy

Resource Requirements (25% weight)
- Capital needs for milestones
- Technical and business talent
- Time to market and revenue
- Resource access assessment
```

**Usability Strengths:**
- **Quantitative Scoring**: 1-10 scale with clear 7+ threshold for viability
- **Balanced Weighting**: Equal 25% weights prevent single-factor bias
- **Specific Questions**: Each category includes concrete evaluation criteria
- **Actionable Outcomes**: Clear decision framework for opportunity pursuit
