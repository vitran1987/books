## Phase 1: Product Foundation Setup (Weeks 1-4)

### Week 1: Team Formation and Process Setup

**Day 1-2: Core Team Assembly**
- **Product Manager Assignment**: Assign experienced product manager with AI product experience
- **Technical Lead Selection**: Select technical lead with AI/ML expertise and product development experience
- **UX Designer Assignment**: Assign UX designer with experience in AI product interfaces
- **Data Scientist/ML Engineer**: Assign AI/ML specialist for model development and optimization
- **Software Engineer**: Assign full-stack engineer for product development and integration

**Day 3-4: Process and Tool Setup**
- **Development Methodology**: Establish agile development process adapted for AI products
- **Communication Tools**: Set up Slack, Microsoft Teams, or similar for team communication
- **Project Management**: Set up Jira, Asana, or similar for project tracking and management
- **Documentation Platform**: Establish Confluence, Notion, or similar for documentation
- **Version Control**: Set up Git repositories for code, models, and documentation

**Day 5: Stakeholder Alignment**
- **Stakeholder Mapping**: Identify and map all key stakeholders and decision-makers
- **Expectation Setting**: Align stakeholders on timeline, scope, and success criteria
- **Communication Plan**: Establish regular communication cadence and reporting structure
- **Decision Authority**: Clarify decision-making authority and escalation processes
- **Resource Commitment**: Confirm resource allocation and team availability

### Week 2: Market Research and User Discovery

**Day 1-2: Market Analysis**
- **Market Size Assessment**: Conduct detailed market sizing and opportunity analysis
- **Competitive Landscape**: Map competitive landscape and analyze existing solutions
- **Technology Trends**: Research relevant AI technology trends and capabilities
- **Regulatory Environment**: Assess regulatory requirements and compliance needs
- **Market Timing**: Evaluate market timing and readiness for AI solution

**Day 3-4: User Research**
- **User Interview Planning**: Plan and schedule user interviews with target customers
- **Interview Execution**: Conduct 10-15 user interviews to understand needs and pain points
- **Survey Deployment**: Deploy broader survey to validate findings from interviews
- **Persona Development**: Develop detailed user personas based on research findings
- **Journey Mapping**: Create current-state user journey maps for target use cases

**Day 5: Research Synthesis**
- **Findings Analysis**: Analyze and synthesize research findings and insights
- **Problem Definition**: Clearly define the problem to be solved with AI
- **Opportunity Assessment**: Assess the opportunity size and potential impact
- **Solution Hypothesis**: Develop initial solution hypothesis and approach
- **Success Criteria**: Define success criteria and key performance indicators

### Week 3: Product Strategy and Requirements

**Day 1-2: Product Strategy Development**
- **Vision and Mission**: Develop clear product vision and mission statements
- **Value Proposition**: Define unique value proposition and competitive differentiation
- **Target Market**: Define target market segments and go-to-market strategy
- **Business Model**: Define business model and monetization strategy
- **Success Metrics**: Define key success metrics and measurement framework

**Day 3-4: Requirements Definition**
- **Functional Requirements**: Define detailed functional requirements for AI product
- **Non-Functional Requirements**: Define performance, security, and scalability requirements
- **AI-Specific Requirements**: Define AI model performance and behavior requirements
- **Integration Requirements**: Define integration requirements with existing systems
- **Compliance Requirements**: Define regulatory and compliance requirements

**Day 5: Requirements Validation**
- **Stakeholder Review**: Review requirements with key stakeholders and decision-makers
- **Technical Feasibility**: Validate technical feasibility with engineering team
- **User Validation**: Validate requirements with target users through interviews or surveys
- **Business Validation**: Validate business case and ROI projections
- **Requirements Finalization**: Finalize and document approved requirements

### Week 4: Technical Architecture and Planning

**Day 1-2: Technical Architecture Design**
- **System Architecture**: Design overall system architecture and component structure
- **AI Architecture**: Design AI model architecture and training/inference pipeline
- **Data Architecture**: Design data collection, storage, and processing architecture
- **Integration Architecture**: Design integration architecture with external systems
- **Security Architecture**: Design security and privacy protection architecture

**Day 3-4: Technology Stack Selection**
- **AI/ML Framework**: Select appropriate AI/ML frameworks and tools
- **Development Stack**: Select development languages, frameworks, and tools
- **Infrastructure Platform**: Select cloud platform and infrastructure services
- **Database Technology**: Select appropriate database and storage technologies
- **Monitoring and Analytics**: Select monitoring, logging, and analytics tools

**Day 5: Development Planning**
- **Project Timeline**: Develop detailed project timeline with milestones and dependencies
- **Resource Planning**: Plan resource allocation and team assignments
- **Risk Assessment**: Identify and assess project risks and mitigation strategies
- **Quality Plan**: Develop quality assurance and testing plan
- **Launch Planning**: Develop initial launch and go-to-market planning

## Phase 2: Product Development (Weeks 5-16)

### Week 5-8: MVP Development Sprint 1

**Sprint 1 Objectives**: Core AI functionality and basic user interface

**Week 5: Data Foundation**
- **Data Collection**: Implement data collection and ingestion systems
- **Data Cleaning**: Develop data cleaning and preprocessing pipelines
- **Data Storage**: Set up data storage and management systems
- **Data Quality**: Implement data quality monitoring and validation
- **Data Pipeline**: Create automated data pipeline for model training

**Week 6: AI Model Development**
- **Model Selection**: Select and implement initial AI model architecture
- **Training Pipeline**: Develop model training and validation pipeline
- **Model Evaluation**: Implement model evaluation and performance measurement
- **Model Optimization**: Initial model optimization and hyperparameter tuning
- **Model Versioning**: Set up model versioning and experiment tracking

**Week 7: Core Application Development**
- **Backend Development**: Develop core backend services and APIs
- **AI Integration**: Integrate AI models with backend services
- **Database Implementation**: Implement database schema and data access layer
- **API Development**: Develop RESTful APIs for frontend integration
- **Authentication**: Implement user authentication and authorization

**Week 8: Basic UI Development**
- **UI Framework**: Set up frontend framework and development environment
- **Core Screens**: Develop core user interface screens and navigation
- **AI Interaction**: Implement basic AI interaction and result display
- **User Input**: Implement user input forms and validation
- **Responsive Design**: Ensure responsive design across devices

### Week 9-12: MVP Development Sprint 2

**Sprint 2 Objectives**: Enhanced AI capabilities and improved user experience

**Week 9: AI Enhancement**
- **Model Improvement**: Improve AI model performance and accuracy
- **Feature Engineering**: Implement advanced feature engineering techniques
- **Model Ensemble**: Implement model ensemble methods if applicable
- **Performance Optimization**: Optimize model inference performance
- **Error Handling**: Implement robust error handling for AI operations

**Week 10: UX Enhancement**
- **User Experience**: Enhance user experience based on initial testing
- **AI Transparency**: Implement AI explanation and transparency features
- **User Feedback**: Implement user feedback collection mechanisms
- **Progressive Disclosure**: Implement progressive disclosure patterns
- **Accessibility**: Implement accessibility features and compliance

**Week 11: Integration and Testing**
- **System Integration**: Complete integration of all system components
- **End-to-End Testing**: Conduct comprehensive end-to-end testing
- **Performance Testing**: Test system performance under realistic load
- **Security Testing**: Conduct security testing and vulnerability assessment
- **User Testing**: Conduct initial user testing with target users

**Week 12: MVP Finalization**
- **Bug Fixes**: Address bugs and issues identified during testing
- **Performance Tuning**: Fine-tune system performance and optimization
- **Documentation**: Complete technical and user documentation
- **Deployment Preparation**: Prepare for MVP deployment and launch
- **Launch Planning**: Finalize MVP launch plan and timeline

### Week 13-16: Beta Testing and Iteration

**Beta Testing Objectives**: Validate product-market fit and gather user feedback

**Week 13: Beta Launch Preparation**
- **Beta Environment**: Set up beta testing environment and infrastructure
- **Beta User Recruitment**: Recruit and onboard beta testing users
- **Feedback Systems**: Implement comprehensive feedback collection systems
- **Analytics Setup**: Set up product analytics and user behavior tracking
- **Support Preparation**: Prepare customer support for beta testing

**Week 14-15: Beta Testing Execution**
- **Beta Launch**: Launch beta version to selected users
- **User Onboarding**: Onboard beta users and provide training
- **Feedback Collection**: Actively collect user feedback and usage data
- **Issue Resolution**: Rapidly resolve issues and bugs reported by beta users
- **Performance Monitoring**: Monitor system performance and user behavior

**Week 16: Beta Analysis and Iteration**
- **Feedback Analysis**: Analyze user feedback and usage data
- **Performance Assessment**: Assess AI model and system performance
- **Product-Market Fit**: Evaluate product-market fit and user satisfaction
- **Iteration Planning**: Plan product iterations based on beta feedback
- **Launch Decision**: Make go/no-go decision for public launch

## Phase 3: Product Launch and Optimization (Weeks 17-24)

### Week 17-20: Launch Preparation and Execution

**Launch Objectives**: Successfully launch AI product to target market

**Week 17: Launch Preparation**
- **Production Environment**: Set up and test production environment
- **Scalability Testing**: Test system scalability and performance under load
- **Security Hardening**: Complete security hardening and compliance validation
- **Launch Marketing**: Prepare marketing materials and launch campaign
- **Support Training**: Train customer support team on product features

**Week 18: Soft Launch**
- **Limited Release**: Launch to limited audience for final validation
- **Performance Monitoring**: Monitor system performance and stability
- **User Feedback**: Collect feedback from early adopters
- **Issue Resolution**: Rapidly resolve any issues or problems
- **Launch Readiness**: Validate readiness for full public launch

**Week 19: Public Launch**
- **Marketing Campaign**: Execute marketing campaign and public announcement
- **User Onboarding**: Implement systematic user onboarding process
- **Customer Support**: Provide comprehensive customer support
- **Performance Monitoring**: Monitor system performance and user adoption
- **Feedback Collection**: Collect user feedback and satisfaction data

**Week 20: Launch Optimization**
- **Performance Tuning**: Optimize system performance based on real usage
- **User Experience**: Optimize user experience based on user behavior
- **Conversion Optimization**: Optimize user conversion and adoption rates
- **Support Optimization**: Optimize customer support processes
- **Marketing Optimization**: Optimize marketing campaigns and messaging

### Week 21-24: Post-Launch Optimization

**Optimization Objectives**: Optimize product performance and user satisfaction

**Week 21-22: Performance Analysis**
- **Usage Analytics**: Analyze user behavior and product usage patterns
- **Performance Metrics**: Analyze AI model and system performance metrics
- **Business Metrics**: Analyze business metrics and ROI
- **User Satisfaction**: Analyze user satisfaction and feedback
- **Competitive Analysis**: Analyze competitive response and market position

**Week 23-24: Product Iteration**
- **Feature Prioritization**: Prioritize new features and improvements based on data
- **Development Planning**: Plan next development iteration and timeline
- **Resource Allocation**: Allocate resources for ongoing development and optimization
- **Roadmap Update**: Update product roadmap based on learnings and feedback
- **Success Celebration**: Celebrate launch success and team achievements
