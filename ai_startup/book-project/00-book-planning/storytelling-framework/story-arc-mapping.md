- **Industry**: Professional Services (Legal Technology)
- **Founded**: 2022 by Winston Weinberg and Gabriel Pereyra
- **Business Model**: AI assistant for legal professionals
- **Key Journey**: From legal research tool to comprehensive legal AI ($80M funding)

#### 4. Grammarly (Writing Enhancement AI)
- **Industry**: Consumer/Prosumer Productivity Tools
- **Founded**: 2009 by Alex Shevchenko, Max Lytvyn, Dmytro Lider
- **Business Model**: Freemium writing enhancement platform
- **Key Journey**: From grammar checker to comprehensive writing assistant ($13B valuation)
