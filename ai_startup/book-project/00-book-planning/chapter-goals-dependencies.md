## üìù Content Development Guidelines

### Language and Style Requirements
- **Simple Professional Language**: All complex concepts explained in easy-to-understand terms
- **Accessible Writing**: Technical jargon translated into everyday business language
- **Conversational Tone**: Writing as if explaining to a colleague using clear, direct language
- **Active Voice**: Clear, engaging, and actionable language throughout

### AI Application Storytelling Requirements
- **Featured Company Focus**: Every chapter follows Jasper AI, Gong.io, Harvey AI, and Grammarly through the same stage of development
- **Continuous Narratives**: Each company's story continues from chapter to chapter, building a complete journey
- **Cross-Industry Insights**: Show how the same challenges are solved differently across content, sales, legal, and writing industries
- **Real Implementation Details**: Include specific technical and business decisions made by each company
- **Lessons Learned Integration**: Extract practical lessons from each company's successes and failures

### Content Structure Standards
- **Story-First Approach**: Begin each chapter with where our featured companies are in their journey
- **Parallel Development**: Show how all four companies handle the same challenge in their unique ways
- **Practical Application**: Extract actionable frameworks from the companies' real experiences
- **Cross-Reference Learning**: Connect lessons across companies to show universal principles
- **AI Application Focus**: Emphasize using AI as a tool rather than building AI platforms

### Dependency Levels
- **Level 0**: Foundation chapters (no dependencies)
- **Level 1**: Basic concepts (depends on Level 0)
- **Level 2**: Applied concepts (depends on Level 0-1)
- **Level 3**: Advanced strategies (depends on Level 0-2)
- **Level 4**: Implementation (depends on all previous levels)
