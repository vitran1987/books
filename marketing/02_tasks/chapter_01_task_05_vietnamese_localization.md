## Content Creation Guidelines

### Vietnamese Adaptation Approach

**Cultural Context Integration:**
The Vietnamese version must go beyond direct translation to provide meaningful cultural adaptation that resonates with Vietnamese business professionals. This includes incorporating relevant Vietnamese market examples where appropriate, adapting business scenarios to reflect Vietnamese organizational structures and decision-making processes, and ensuring that implementation guidance considers Vietnamese business culture and practices.

**Professional Vietnamese Writing Standards:**
All content must adhere to professional Vietnamese business writing conventions, using appropriate formal language structures and business terminology that Vietnamese professionals expect from authoritative publications. The writing should demonstrate the same level of expertise and authority as the English version while feeling natural and engaging to Vietnamese readers.

**Technical Accuracy with Cultural Relevance:**
Technical concepts and marketing strategies must be explained with Vietnamese business context in mind, including how international platforms and technologies apply to Vietnamese markets. Implementation examples should consider Vietnamese business practices and regulatory environment while maintaining global applicability.
