## Research Requirements

### Primary Research (6-7 hours)
1. **Technology Roadmap Analysis**
   - Review platform development roadmaps from major vendors
   - Analyze patent filings and R&D investment patterns
   - Study beta program announcements and early access features
   - Document technology maturity cycles and adoption timelines

2. **Investment and Funding Analysis**
   - Compile VC investment data in marketing technology (2023-2025)
   - Analyze acquisition patterns and strategic partnerships
   - Review public company earnings calls and strategic announcements
   - Study startup funding rounds and emerging technology focus areas

3. **Expert Prediction Interviews**
   - Conduct 4-5 interviews with industry analysts and thought leaders
   - Focus on 3-5 year predictions and confidence levels
   - Document reasoning behind predictions and supporting evidence
   - Gather insights on potential disruptions and market shifts

### Secondary Research (3-4 hours)
1. **Regulatory Development Tracking**
   - Monitor privacy regulation evolution (GDPR, CCPA, emerging laws)
   - Analyze AI regulation proposals and potential marketing impact
   - Study antitrust investigations and platform regulation trends
   - Review international regulatory convergence patterns

2. **Academic and Think Tank Research**
   - Latest research on consumer behavior evolution
   - Technology adoption studies and prediction models
   - Economic impact analysis of digital marketing trends
   - Demographic and generational shift implications

### Predictive Survey Development (2-3 hours)
1. **Future Trends Survey Creation**
   - 25-question survey on technology adoption intentions and timeline predictions
   - Target 200+ senior marketing professionals and technology leaders
   - Include confidence scoring and reasoning for predictions
   - Segment by company size, industry, and geographic region
