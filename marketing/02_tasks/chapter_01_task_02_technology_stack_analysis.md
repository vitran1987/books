## Research Requirements

### Primary Research (5-6 hours)
1. **Technology Platform Testing**
   - Hands-on evaluation of 5-7 major marketing platforms
   - Document current capabilities, limitations, and integration options
   - Test API functionality and data export capabilities
   - Analyze pricing models and ROI potential

2. **Vendor Interviews and Briefings**
   - Schedule briefings with 3-4 major MarTech vendors
   - Focus on roadmap developments and integration capabilities
   - Document new features and competitive positioning
   - Gather case studies and implementation examples

3. **Client Implementation Case Studies**
   - Review 3-5 recent technology implementations from personal experience
   - Document challenges faced, solutions implemented, and results achieved
   - Analyze ROI data and performance improvements
   - Identify common patterns and best practices

### Secondary Research (3-4 hours)
1. **Industry Analysis and Reports**
   - Latest MarTech landscape reports (Scott Brinker, Forrester, Gartner)
   - Technology adoption surveys and benchmarking studies
   - Integration platform analysis and vendor comparisons
   - ROI measurement methodology research

2. **Competitive Intelligence**
   - Recent acquisitions and partnerships in MarTech space
   - Platform feature releases and capability expansions
   - Pricing model evolution and market positioning
   - Customer satisfaction and retention data

### Advanced Survey Deployment (2-3 hours)
1. **Technology Stack Survey Creation**
   - 20-question comprehensive survey on current technology usage
   - Focus on integration challenges, ROI measurement, and future plans
   - Target 150+ marketing technology professionals
   - Include detailed demographic and company size segmentation
