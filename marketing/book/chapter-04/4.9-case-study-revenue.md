# 4.9 Case Study - Phân Tích Các Website Thu Nhập $5k-$50k/Tháng

Spreadsheet hiển thị con số khiến Patrick Reynolds không thể tin được: $47,234 revenue từ Google Adsense trong tháng vừa qua, từ một website về finance tips mà anh bắt đầu như side project ba năm trước. Không phải influencer, không có team lớn, chỉ là một người với laptop và commitment tạo quality content consistently. Journey của Patrick từ $0 đến gần $50k/tháng không phải magic mà là application có hệ thống các principles đã discuss trong chương này, combined với patience, consistency, và smart optimization dựa trên data. Analyzing các publishers thành công ở các revenue tiers khác nhau—từ $5k đến $50k mỗi tháng—reveals patterns có thể học và replicate, cũng như realities về effort, time, và resources required để reach từng level. Những case studies này không phải exceptional outliers mà là representative examples của thousands publishers worldwide đang build sustainable ad-based businesses, providing roadmap rõ ràng cho anyone willing to put in work.

## Case Study 1: The Budget Mom ($5,200/tháng từ 420,000 pageviews)

Kumiko Love, người behind The Budget Mom blog, bắt đầu tháng 1 năm 2017 để document personal journey out of debt và share budgeting strategies. Không có formal background trong finance hoặc journalism, Kumiko chỉ có passion về personal finance và desire help người khác. Năm đầu tiên là struggle—total revenue chưa đến $800 cho cả năm, với majority tháng earning $20-50. Traffic chủ yếu từ Pinterest, averaging 15,000-25,000 pageviews/tháng. Turning point đến tháng 8 năm 2018 khi một article về "cash envelope budgeting system" viral trên Pinterest, driving 120,000 pageviews trong một tháng và $420 Adsense revenue. Kumiko realized potential và doubled down: increased publishing frequency từ 2 lên 4 articles/tuần, optimized Pinterest strategy với Tailwind scheduling, và focused heavy vào SEO optimization cho existing content.

Tháng 6 năm 2020, The Budget Mom đạt consistent $5,000+/tháng Adsense revenue với 400,000-450,000 pageviews. Key strategies Kumiko employed: content focus trên long-tail keywords với commercial intent như "how to budget on $30k salary" hay "best budgeting apps for couples" thay vì generic "budgeting tips." Các articles dài (2,500-4,000 words) với comprehensive coverage, attracting backlinks tự nhiên và ranking well. Pinterest optimization aggressive—30-40 pins mỗi ngày pointing to best content, using rich pins và seasonal content strategy. Ad placement strategic: two above-fold placements (one sidebar, one in-content sau first paragraph), two mid-content, one end-of-article, một sticky footer mobile. Auto ads disabled để maintain control và avoid over-saturation. RPM averaging $12-13, slightly above niche average ($10-11 for personal finance) nhờ high-intent traffic và good ad placement.

Demographics quan trọng: 78% traffic US-based, 68% female aged 25-44, majority có household income $40k-80k—prime target cho financial services advertisers willing to pay premium CPMs. Engagement metrics strong: average time on page 3:20, bounce rate 52%, pages per session 1.8. Kumiko attributes success đến "consistency over perfection"—publishing regularly thậm chí khi content không perfect, building momentum và trust với both audience và Google algorithm. Challenges faced: seasonal fluctuations (revenue cao hơn 30-40% tháng 1-2 khi people focus vào New Year resolutions, thấp hơn 20% summer months), Google algorithm updates occasionally hurting traffic 10-20% temporarily requiring content refresh, và increasing competition in personal finance niche making newer articles harder to rank.

Growth trajectory instructive: Year 1 (2017): $780 total revenue, average 18,000 pageviews/tháng. Year 2 (2018): $8,400 total revenue, average 55,000 pageviews/tháng. Year 3 (2019): $28,000 total revenue, average 190,000 pageviews/tháng. Year 4 (2020): $61,000 total revenue, average 420,000 pageviews/tháng, reaching $5,200/tháng average. Time investment: Year 1-2 là 15-20 giờ/tuần (evenings và weekends while working full-time), Year 3 là 30-35 giờ/tuần (quit full-time job tháng 4 năm 2019), Year 4 là 25-30 giờ/tuần (more efficient với systems in place). Key lesson từ Kumiko: "Don't expect quick results. First two years I made almost nothing. Năm thứ ba mọi thứ bắt đầu compound—content library lớn đủ để drive consistent traffic, backlinks tích lũy, domain authority tăng. Patience và consistency là essential. Most people quit in year one khi seeing zero results."

## Case Study 2: StoryLearning với Dr. Olly Richards ($18,500/tháng từ 1.1M pageviews)

Dr. Olly Richards, linguistics PhD và polyglot speaking tám languages, launch StoryLearning.com tháng 9 năm 2015 với concept unique: teach foreign languages qua stories thay vì traditional grammar drills. Website cung cấp short stories ở different languages với translations, grammar explanations, và audio recordings. Model ban đầu là paid courses và books, với blog content như content marketing driving awareness. Adsense được add tháng 3 năm 2016 như "bonus revenue stream," không expect nhiều. Initial months disappointing: $80-150/tháng từ 40,000-60,000 pageviews, RPM chỉ $2-2.5 vì language learning niche có CPMs thấp và majority traffic từ tier-2/tier-3 countries (Brazil, Mexico, Poland, etc.).

Breakthrough đến năm 2018 khi Olly realized potential scale blog content significantly. Hired hai writers part-time, scaling từ 2 articles/tuần lên 12-15 articles/tuần covering nhiều languages (Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Russian, Japanese, Korean, Chinese) và topics (grammar guides, vocabulary lists, cultural articles, learning tips). Investment: ~$3,000/tháng cho writers, editors, và occasional native speaker reviewers. Traffic exploded: từ 80,000 pageviews/tháng tháng 1 năm 2018 lên 450,000 tháng 12 năm 2018, lên 900,000 tháng 12 năm 2019, và stabilizing around 1.0-1.2M pageviews/tháng năm 2020-2021. Adsense revenue trajectory tương ứng: $180/tháng → $6,800/tháng (năm 2018) → $15,200/tháng (năm 2019) → $18,500/tháng average (năm 2020-2021).

RPM improved từ $2.50 lên $16-17 qua optimization aggressive. Key changes: shifted content strategy focus more vào commercial-intent keywords targeting US/UK learners (ví dụ: "best Spanish courses," "how to learn French fast for travel," "business German phrases") rather than pure educational content. Các articles này attract tier-1 traffic và higher-paying advertisers (language learning apps, travel companies, online course platforms). Ad placement refined qua extensive A/B testing: removed sidebar ads (performed poorly trên language learning content), focused on in-content placements—one sau introduction, one every 800-1,000 words trong long articles, one above comments section. Enabled Google matched content recommendation units at article ends, generating surprisingly high CTR (4-6%) và strong RPMs ($12-18). Mobile optimization critical—70% traffic là mobile, implemented AMP cho key articles, improved mobile page speed từ 4.2 giây xuống 2.1 giây, resulting bounce rate giảm từ 67% xuống 48% trên mobile.

Diversification của traffic sources helped quality và stability: 55% organic search (Google), 20% direct (email subscribers và returning visitors), 15% social (primarily YouTube channel cross-promotion và Facebook group), 10% referral (language learning forums, partner sites). Challenges significant: operating across nhiều languages requires careful quality control—mistakes trong grammar explanations hoặc translations hurt credibility. Google algorithm updates impact different language verticals differently—German content might spike while Spanish drops. Ad CPMs vary dramatically by language: English-language articles về learning Spanish (US audience) earn $18-22 RPM, while Portuguese-language content về learning English (Brazilian audience) earn $4-6 RPM. Balancing content production across languages để optimize overall revenue là ongoing strategic challenge.

Key innovation: Olly implemented "hub-and-spoke" content model. "Hub" pages là comprehensive guides (ví dụ: "Complete Guide to Spanish Grammar") ultra-detailed, 8,000-15,000 words, targeting high-volume keywords. "Spoke" pages là specific topics (ví dụ: "Spanish Subjunctive Mood Explained," "Por vs Para in Spanish") linking back to hub, targeting long-tail keywords. Hub pages rank competitively và attract backlinks, spokes capture long-tail traffic và channel to hubs, increasing pages per session từ 1.3 lên 2.1 và boosting overall revenue. Lesson từ Olly: "Adsense started as afterthought nhưng became significant revenue stream—$18k/tháng pays for entire content team với surplus. Key là treating it seriously: optimize placements, test continuously, monitor metrics, invest in content quality và SEO. Many content creators leave money on table vì not optimizing monetization với same rigor they optimize content."

## Case Study 3: The Points Guy ($42,000/tháng từ 2.8M pageviews)

Brian Kelly launch ThePointsGuy.com năm 2010 sau years working như consultant helping clients maximize credit card rewards và airline miles. Initial concept đơn giản: share strategies free publicly, monetize qua affiliate commissions từ credit card signups. Blog rapidly gained traction trong travel hacking community. Tháng 6 năm 2011, Brian thêm Adsense như supplementary revenue. First month: $1,240 từ 180,000 pageviews—surprisingly high RPM $6.90 nhờ premium travel audience và financial services advertisers. Brian recognized Adsense potential và began optimizing seriously alongside affiliate focus.

Tháng 10 năm 2015, The Points Guy đạt landmark: $1 million total revenue trong một tháng, với Adsense contributing $42,000 từ 2.8M pageviews (RPM $15). Scale đến level này required transformation từ one-person blog thành professional media operation. Brian hired team: managing editor, 4 staff writers, 6 freelance contributors, technical team, và dedicated ad ops person. Monthly content burn rate: ~$35,000 cho salaries, freelancers, travel expenses for reviews, tools/subscriptions. Revenue justification: Adsense $42k + affiliate commissions $850k+ + sponsored content $120k = $1M+ monthly revenue gross.

Adsense optimization ở scale này sophisticated. Ad ops manager A/B tests continuously: different ad sizes, positions, colors, auto-ads vs manual placements. Best performers: large rectangle (336x280) placements sau first 200 words trong articles, in-content native ads blending với article style, sticky anchor ads trên mobile (carefully implemented to avoid policy violations), matched content recommendation blocks at article ends. Header bidding implemented tháng 3 năm 2016 via Prebid.js với 6 demand partners (AppNexus, Index Exchange, Rubicon, OpenX, PubMatic, Amazon TAM), increasing RPM từ $15 lên $21 (40% boost) mà chỉ tăng page load time 0.3 giây. Ad refresh implemented cautiously cho long-form articles (travel guides, comprehensive points reviews) với 90-giây intervals, max 2 refreshes, adding estimated 15-20% revenue trên refreshed pages.

Content strategy highly SEO-focused targeting commercial keywords với massive search volume: "best travel credit cards 2024," "how to use Chase Ultimate Rewards," "United Airlines award chart," "American Express Platinum review." Articles extremely comprehensive—3,000-8,000 words, regularly updated, với detailed comparison tables, calculators, và expert analysis. Investment in tools và data: subscriptions to SEMrush, Ahrefs, custom-built award availability tracking tools, partnerships với flight search APIs. Backlink strategy aggressive: digital PR campaigns, partnerships với major travel publications, guest posting, awards và rankings ("Best Travel Blogs") generating natural links. Domain authority reached DA 82 by 2021, enabling competitive ranking cho highly contested keywords.

Audience demographics critical đến high RPMs: 72% US-based, household income median $120k+, 45% có college degrees, 62% travel for business regularly. Đây là exactly audience credit card companies, airlines, hotels, và premium travel services want to reach, driving CPMs $25-40 cho targeted placements. Engagement metrics exceptional cho niche: average time on page 4:20, bounce rate chỉ 38%, pages per session 2.6—indicating highly engaged audience actively consuming multiple articles, perfect cho ad monetization. Email list 1.2M subscribers drives significant direct traffic (20% total), reducing reliance on Google và providing stable revenue base.

Challenges at this scale: intense competition—dozens of similar sites launched copying model, requiring constant innovation và content freshness to maintain rankings. Google algorithm updates can swing traffic ±15-25% month-over-month, requiring constant adaptation. Ad policy compliance với complex setup (header bidding, refresh, multiple networks) requires ongoing monitoring—any mistake could jeopardize $40k+/tháng revenue stream. Seasonal fluctuations significant: revenue peaks tháng 1-2 và tháng 11-12 (credit card signup season, holiday travel planning), dips 20-30% tháng 8-9. Managing team và maintaining quality at scale while preserving founder's voice và expertise ongoing challenge.

Key lesson từ Brian Kelly: "Scale requires different mindset than starting out. At $5k/tháng, you can DIY everything. At $40k/tháng, you need team, systems, specialization. I had to learn to delegate—hire writers better than me at certain topics, trust ad ops person to optimize monetization, invest in tools và infrastructure. ROI mindset critical: will $5k investment in this content/tool/person generate $10k additional revenue? Many publishers stay stuck ở $5-10k/tháng vì afraid to invest và scale. But calculated risks và reinvestment của profits vào growth là how you reach next level."

## Case Study 4: Apartment Therapy ($28,000/tháng từ 3.5M pageviews)

Maxwell Ryan, trained architect frustrated với corporate architecture firms, start Apartment Therapy năm 2001 như email newsletter offering decorating advice cho small urban apartments. Website launched năm 2004, initially như companion to newsletter và in-person decorating services Maxwell offered ở New York. Adsense added năm 2006 khi traffic reached 50,000 pageviews/tháng, generating modest $180-250/tháng. Năm 2007-2009 là turning point: Maxwell commit to building Apartment Therapy into full-fledged publication, publishing daily instead of weekly, expanding beyond decorating vào broader home lifestyle topics (cooking, organizing, DIY, sustainable living), và beginning to build team.

Tháng 5 năm 2012, Apartment Therapy reached significant milestone: $25,000 Adsense revenue trong một tháng từ 3.2M pageviews. Tháng 10 năm 2014, peak tháng hit $31,200 từ 3.8M pageviews. Average năm 2013-2015 settled around $28,000/tháng từ 3.5M pageviews, với RPM $7.50-8.50—lower than premium finance/travel niches nhưng solid cho lifestyle content. Key đến success: extremely loyal audience returning nhiều lần, driving pages per session lên 3.2 (exceptional) và time on site lên 6:40 average. Repeat visitors account for 45% traffic, indicating strong brand và content quality.

Content strategy focused on "aspirational but achievable"—showcasing beautiful homes và clever solutions attainable cho readers với average budgets, not just luxury design out of reach. House tours consistently top performers: real apartments và homes của real people (not professional designers), với budgets và sources listed, attracting massive engagement. Before/after transformations viral potential: dramatic makeovers với detailed process documentation và cost breakdowns. DIY tutorials step-by-step với clear photos, shopping lists, và realistic time estimates. Seasonal content calendar ensuring relevant topics year-round: spring cleaning guides tháng 3-4, outdoor living tháng 5-7, back-to-school organizing tháng 8-9, holiday decorating tháng 10-12.

Ad strategy prioritized user experience highly—Maxwell conscious rằng loyal audience is core asset, không muốn alienate với aggressive monetization. Conservative ad load: 3-4 ad units per article (most publishers trong niche chạy 6-8), favoring native ad formats blending với content style, strict policy against pop-ups hoặc interstitials (common trong lifestyle niche nhưng user-hostile). Auto ads disabled entirely—manual control ensuring ads placed thoughtfully, không interrupting visual flow của photo-heavy content. Result: lower RPM than competitors running more ads ($8 vs $12-15 typical) nhưng much higher user satisfaction, retention, và long-term value. Maxwell's philosophy: "I'd rather earn $8 RPM với happy loyal audience growing 20% yearly than $15 RPM với annoyed audience declining 10% yearly. Long-term math favors sustainable approach."

Revenue diversification sophisticated: Adsense $28k/tháng provided foundation, nhưng supplemented by direct-sold sponsorships ($40-60k/tháng, higher margins), affiliate commissions từ product recommendations ($25-35k/tháng), và online courses/ebooks ($10-15k/tháng). Total revenue $110-150k/tháng supported team of 12: editor-in-chief, 6 writers/editors, 2 photographers, social media manager, ad ops/business person, technical admin. Reinvestment heavy: 60-70% revenue back vào team, content production, site improvements, tools. Profit margin 30-40% after all costs, reasonable cho media business.

Technical infrastructure investment significant: custom WordPress theme optimized cho image-heavy content với lazy loading và CDN (Cloudflare), mobile-first design (72% traffic mobile by 2015), Pinterest optimization built-in (30% traffic từ Pinterest at peak), email capture strategically placed capturing 8-12% visitors to newsletter (1.8M subscribers by 2018). Site speed maintained under 2.5 giây despite heavy images through aggressive optimization: WebP format, responsive images, HTTP/2, above-fold prioritization. Speed critical cho both SEO và ad viewability—slow sites lose traffic và ad revenue simultaneously.

Challenges: home/lifestyle niche extremely competitive với both established publications (Better Homes & Gardens, HGTV) và wave of blogger competitors. Differentiation through authentic voice, real homes, và community focus critical. Seasonal traffic swings 35-40%—tháng 1 và tháng 9 peak, tháng 7-8 dip. Ad CPMs vary seasonally—high Q4 (holiday advertisers), low Q3 (summer slump). Managing team và maintaining editorial quality và voice consistency while scaling challenging. Balancing revenue optimization với user experience ongoing tension requiring constant evaluation.

Lesson từ Maxwell: "Build audience first, monetization second. First 5 years, I focused purely on creating best content và building loyal audience. Monetization came naturally khi traffic và engagement were strong. Many publishers reverse này—optimize monetization trước having solid audience foundation—và struggle. Your audience is asset; treat them well, give value, và monetization will follow. Also, diversify—Adsense is great foundation nhưng don't rely on it exclusively. Multiple revenue streams create stability và maximize value từ traffic you're working hard to build."

## Case Study 5: MoneySavingExpert.com ($12,000/tháng từ 800,000 pageviews)

Martin Lewis launch MoneySavingExpert.com năm 2003 ở UK sau career như financial journalist frustrated vì mainstream media không serving consumers' actual needs. Mission rõ ràng: provide totally unbiased money-saving advice không bị influence bởi advertisers or affiliates. Radical approach for time: refuse affiliate commissions từ product recommendations, rely purely on display ads (primarily Adsense) cho revenue. Initial years tough financially—Martin work journalist jobs bên cạnh để pay bills while building site. Adsense revenue year 1-2: £150-400/tháng (khoảng $200-550), barely covering hosting và minimal expenses.

Breakthrough đến năm 2005-2006 khi Martin's detailed guides về switching utilities, finding best credit cards, và reclaiming bank charges went viral trong UK media và được featured trên BBC, ITV. Traffic spiked từ 60,000 lên 500,000 pageviews/tháng trong 6 tháng. Tháng 11 năm 2007, site reached 800,000 pageviews và £8,200 Adsense revenue (khoảng $12,000 USD at time). RPM exceptional: £10.25 ($15-16 USD equivalent), significantly cao hơn typical UK sites ($8-10) vì combination of factors: highly engaged UK audience (tier-1 geo), financial content attracting premium advertisers, và exceptional user engagement metrics (average 5+ minutes on site, 42% bounce rate, 2.8 pages per session).

Content philosophy unique và key đến both audience loyalty và high RPMs: absolutely no affiliate links or sponsored content—every recommendation purely based on merit, extensive research và testing—Martin personally tested dozens of products/services, radical transparency về everything including business model và revenue sources, comprehensive guides updated regularly—some articles 10,000+ words với every detail imaginable, community forum extremely active với 2M+ posts helping each other (not monetized—pure community value), weekly email newsletter to 500k+ subscribers keeping them engaged. Approach built enormous trust—surveys showed 87% readers trusted MoneySavingExpert recommendations more than any other financial source, including traditional media.

Ad implementation deliberately conservative to preserve trust và user experience: maximum 3 ad units per page (industry standard 5-7), clearly labeled ads to avoid confusion với content, no pop-ups, interstitials, or aggressive formats, preference for text ads và native formats over flashy display ads, strict vetting of advertiser categories—no payday loans, no questionable financial products, maintaining editorial integrity even if meant losing revenue. Result: lower ad density than competitors but higher engagement và viewability, translating to premium CPMs. Martin's calculation: "Better to show fewer ads to engaged users than many ads to annoyed users who leave quickly."

Business model attracted major acquisition interest. Năm 2012, MoneySavingExpert.com sold to MoneySuperMarket.com for £87 million ($140M USD), one of largest UK digital media acquisitions. At time of sale, site generating approximately £140,000/tháng total revenue ($215k USD), with roughly £80,000 ($123k) từ display ads including Adsense (balance từ direct partnerships và sponsorships). Adsense portion peaked around £8,000-12,000/tháng ($12-18k USD) depending on seasonality—highest Q4 và Q1 khi financial planning top of mind, lowest Q3 summer months.

Scale strategy focused on depth rather than breadth: thay vì covering mọi financial topics superficially, focus on specific high-impact areas deeply (credit cards, savings accounts, utilities, insurance, banking, mortgages) với ultimate authority. SEO naturally strong vì comprehensive coverage và constant updates meaning Google saw site as definitive source. Backlinks organic—journalists, consumer organizations, government agencies linking as trusted resource. Social strategy minimal—mainly email và word-of-mouth, proving you don't need massive social presence nếu content genuinely valuable. Team deliberately kept lean: 8 full-time staff (researchers, writers, community moderators, tech) plus Martin as editor. Low overhead maximized profitability.

Challenges navigating integrity vs monetization: pressure to add affiliates or sponsored content to increase revenue resisted firmly, ad optimization walked fine line—maximize revenue without compromising experience, Google algorithm updates less impact than most sites vì strong brand và direct traffic, but still required adaptation, maintaining accuracy và updates across hundreds of detailed guides labor-intensive, managing active community demanding—moderation, responses, maintaining positive culture. UK market specific differences: VAT on digital ads reducing net revenue, GDPR impacting targeting và CPMs slightly, cultural expectations about advertising different than US.

Key lessons từ Martin Lewis: "Trust is everything. Short-term revenue temptations—affiliates, aggressive ads, sponsored content—would have destroyed what made site valuable. By staying pure, we built trust that translated to massive engaged audience, which ultimately generated both strong ad revenue và acquisition value. Patience essential—took 4 years to reach significant revenue, 9 years to reach exit. Most people quit too soon. Quality content with genuine value to readers is sustainable moat—algorithms change, ad networks change, nhưng valuable content remains valuable. Focus on readers first, revenue follows."

## Common Patterns Across Success Cases

Analyzing patterns across these case studies reveals consistent themes applicable to publishers at any level. Pattern one: long time horizon—all successful publishers took 2-4+ years to reach significant revenue, requiring patience và sustained effort through lean initial years. Pattern two: content quality và depth over quantity—comprehensive, well-researched, regularly updated content consistently outperformed thin or superficial content. Pattern three: audience-first mindset—prioritizing user experience và trust over aggressive monetization led to better long-term outcomes. Pattern four: data-driven optimization—successful publishers continuously test, measure, và refine based on metrics, not guesses. Pattern five: diversification—multiple traffic sources và revenue streams create stability và reduce risk.

Across different revenue tiers, required effort và investment scales: $5k/tháng level typically achievable solo or with minimal freelance help, 20-30 giờ/tuần, minimal tool investment ($50-200/tháng). $15-20k/tháng typically requires small team hoặc significant freelance investment, 40+ giờ/tuần commitment, moderate tool/infrastructure investment ($500-1,500/tháng). $40k+/tháng requires proper team, systems, specialization, full-time commitment từ founder plus team, significant investment ($5k-15k/tháng operating costs). ROI calculation critical: investments in content, tools, people should generate 2-3x return within 6-12 tháng to be sustainable.

Niche selection impact massive: premium niches (finance, travel, technology, business) support RPMs $12-25+, enabling strong revenue with moderate traffic. General lifestyle, entertainment, hobbies typically $5-10 RPMs requiring higher traffic for same revenue. Educational content varies widely: commercial topics (test prep, professional development) $10-15 RPMs, academic subjects $4-8 RPMs. Geographic audience mix critical: 70%+ tier-1 countries (US, UK, Canada, Australia) necessary cho premium RPMs, high tier-3 traffic caps revenue potential significantly. Traffic quality trumps quantity—better to have 500k engaged pageviews than 2M bot-heavy or bounce-heavy traffic.

Realistic timeline expectations: Year 1: build foundation, learn basics, expect minimal revenue ($0-3k total for year). Year 2: growth phase, content library builds, early traffic momentum, expect $500-2,000/tháng if executing well. Year 3: acceleration if surviving first two years, compound effects kick in, expect $2-8k/tháng range. Year 4+: maturity và optimization, potential to reach $10-30k+/tháng với sustained effort and/or team scaling. These timelines assume consistent effort—sporadic publishing extends timeline significantly. Most failures happen năm 1-2 khi results minimal và patience runs out, but those persisting have disproportionate success năm 3+.
