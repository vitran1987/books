## Success Criteria

✅ Families feel equipped to start  
✅ Clear, step-by-step transition path  
✅ Realistic about challenges and timelines  
✅ Addresses cultural and practical concerns  
✅ Flexible for different family situations

❌ Fails if: Oversimplifying transition, ignoring real barriers, unrealistic timelines, insufficient practical detail

---

**CRITICAL**: Change is hard. Make the path clear, manageable, and encourage starting small.
